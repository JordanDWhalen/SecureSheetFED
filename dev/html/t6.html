Founded in 2003

SecureSheet Technologies, LLC is a privately held company which began in 2003 with a vision for creating a simple way for businesses to communicate more effectively. Leveraging existing technologies, and focusing on easy adoption for users, SecureSheet has transformed the ordinary spreadsheet, into secure, multi-user, web based, business applications.

SecureSheet has successfully helped its customers address a wide variety of needs. From large, multi-national enterprises looking for a quick solution to tackle one-time, short term projects to small or medium sized companies looking to turn their current spreadsheets into full blown, on-going applications, SecureSheet has solved their problems.
