History

SecureSheet Technologies, LLC is a privately held company which began in 2003 with a vision for creating a simple way for businesses to communicate more effectively. Leveraging existing technologies, and focusing on easy adoption for users, SecureSheet has transformed the ordinary spreadsheet, into secure, multi-user, web based, business applications. Our focus is to provide easy adoption and exceptional customer service for our users, and low costs for our clients.

Over the years our clients have used SecureSheet to improve all areas of their business operation, including Sales, Logistics, Supply Chain management and Human Resources.

In the last few years, the majority of our clients use SecureSheet for all areas related to Compensation and Performance Management, specifically Merit Pay and Bonus / Incentive review cycles. For some clients, SecureSheet is their compensation application. For many of our clients, our solution compliments their existing HRIS system, because our clients continue to have areas where they need to collect and manage data outside a standard product.
